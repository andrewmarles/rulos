R-ULAV Procedures Handbook: Draining water condensation from compressor tank

1. Turn off the air compressor.
2. Ensure that the tank valve is open.
3. Open the rocket supply valve.
4. Fire the thrusters / run the reservoir drain sequence until the tank pressure is almost entirely released.
5. Close the rocket supply valve.
6. Verify the stored tank pressure is below 5psi.
7. If tank pressure is higher than 5psi, pull the pressure-release ring on the compressor.

(You can skip steps 3 -- 5 and just use the release ring to drain a full tank, but it makes a really abrupt, loud noise when activated at high pressures.)

8. Place newspaper under the compressor.
Open the tank drain screw.
Rusty water will drip out.
Let it drip for a few minutes.
Gently close the tank drain screw.
9. Turn on the compressor switch.
The compressor will run for a few minutes to restore the tank and pipeline pressure to 115psi.
